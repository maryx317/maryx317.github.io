Waterboy Adventures
Video game


Waterboy Adventures is a puzzle-based game. The main character's village is experiencing a severe drought! Help him gain abilities and replenish the water in his village.

For this collaborative effort, I developed AI pursuit agents, weapon logic, character persistence logic, and treasure chest persistence logic. I also modeled all four different character bodies in Blender.

Lumberjack Jack
Game Boy Advance game


Lumberjack Jack is an escape room game. Jack has lost his way at work one day! Help him get home by obtaining 4 keys to escape.

This was a solo project where I programmed, designed, and drew everything. This was also one of the top 10 projects in the Computer Organization and Programming class of 400+ students.
Escape to Elysium
Board game


The ground is gone. Monsters have destroyed Grealia. A mystical portal is humanity's last hope to escape from these monster's wrath. Explore islands, collect resources, upgrade your ship, and battle mosnters to survive and be the one to Escape to Elysium.

This was a board game created by Golden Swarm Games, a board game studio I started in 2018. Besides leading the studio, I was the leader of design team for this game, easing gameplay for potential players and creating many layouts for the game files.
